About

Vat is developed by the Database Research Group of the University of Marburg (head: Prof. Bernhard Seeger) and supported by the German Research Foundation DFG under the grant agreement number 442032008 (NFDI4Biodiversity). The project is part of NFDI, the National Research Data Infrastructure Programme in Germany. The original design of Vat was a joint collaboration with the Senckenberg Biodiversity and Climate Research Centre (BiK-F) (head: Prof. Thomas Hickler).
